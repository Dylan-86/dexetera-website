What is Dexetera?

Dexetera is a gas-free decentralized exchange (DEX) built on Hyperliquid that enables trading of any measurable market. Unlike traditional DEXs limited to crypto pairs, Dexetera allows users to create and trade custom futures contracts for real-world data like house prices, commodity costs, or social media metrics with complete self-custody and zero gas fees.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about trading on Dexetera

Dexetera allows you to trade any verifiable, measurable market. This includes traditional assets like cryptocurrencies, commodities, and stocks, as well as custom futures contracts you can create yourself. If there's a reliable data source for it, you can trade it on Dexetera.

Dexetera leverages Hyperliquid's high-speed infrastructure to offer gas-free trading. We've optimized our smart contracts and use meta-transactions to cover gas costs, allowing you to execute trades with just one click, without worrying about gas fees.

Yes, Dexetera is fully decentralized. We don't have access to your funds, there are no middlemen. The platform operates with a fully on-chain order book, and all trades are settled directly on the Hyperliquid blockchain.

Creating a custom market on Dexetera is simple. You'll need to define the underlying asset or metric you want to trade, set the contract parameters (including the 1-year expiration), and specify the data source that will be used for settlement. Our interface guides you through the process step-by-step.

You can deposit USDC on the Arbitrum network to trade on Dexetera. We support USDC as our primary trading currency to ensure stability and liquidity.

All trades on Dexetera are settled on-chain through smart contracts. When a futures contract expires, it automatically settles based on the verifiable data source you specified when creating the market. Settlement is transparent, automatic, and recorded on the Hyperliquid blockchain for anyone to verify.
