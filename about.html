Redefining Decentralized Trading

Dexetera was born from a simple belief: trading should be permissionless, transparent, and completely free from middlemen extracting rent. We are building the infrastructure for a truly open financial system.

Our Mission

For too long, the financial trading landscape has been dominated by centralized entities that control access, custodiate funds, and impose arbitrary fees. Even early "decentralized" exchanges suffered from high gas fees, slow execution, and limited market offerings.

At Dexetera, we are leveraging the sheer speed and efficiency of the Hyperliquid blockchain to deliver a zero-compromise trading experience. Our mission is to enable anyone, anywhere, to trade any measurable market—from traditional equities to bespoke crypto indices—instantly, securely, and with zero gas costs.

We believe that Your Keys = Your Markets. Self-custody isn't just a feature; it is the fundamental right of every trader.

Hyperliquid Engine

Utilizing a purpose-built L1 optimized entirely for trading. This provides us with < 1s latency, high throughput, and the ability to process orders exactly as a centralized exchange would, but entirely on-chain.

2

Oracle-less Infrastructure

To support "any measurable market," Dexetera uses external URLs as "data source" to verify the outcome of the market, upon expiration.

3

Open Source Core

Verifiability is key to trust in DeFi. Our smart contracts will be transparently verifiable, heavily audited, and designed with security bounds that mathematically prevent funds from being moved without explicit user signatures.
