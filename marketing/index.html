Executive Summary

Dexetera requires a systematic approach to identify influencers who can effectively communicate the platform's unique value proposition: gasless trading on Hyperliquid infrastructure with complete self-custody and user-generated market capabilities.

The strategy prioritizes micro and mid-tier creators (1K–500K followers) who demonstrate authentic engagement with DeFi-native audiences, have verifiable crypto promotion history, and maintain English-language content standards across multiple platforms.

Through curated list analysis, platform-native discovery, and cross-platform verification, this framework establishes a comprehensive database of 200–300 qualified influencers ready for tiered outreach and performance-based partnership development.

Influencer Tier Specifications

Micro-Tier (1K–50,000 Followers)

  • Exceptional Engagement: 3–5× higher engagement rates due to closer creator-audience relationships and responsive community management
  • Optimal Education: Superior for complex technical messaging about gasless trading and self-custody solutions
  • Cost Efficiency: Partnerships typically require $100–$500 compensation versus $5K+ for mid-tier creators

Mid-Tier (50K–500K Followers)

  • Meaningful Scale: Substantial reach expansion while maintaining accessibility and engagement quality
  • Professional Operations: Established partnership processes, standardized pricing, and reliable deliverables
  • Strategic Impact: Flagship partnerships for major announcements and category leadership positioning

Required Data Fields for Complete Database

  • Full name/pseudonym: Bio analysis, direct inquiry, public profiles
  • Primary platform persona: Primary engagement location
  • Geographic location: Regional targeting, timezone coordination
  • Professional background: Experience verification, credibility assessment
  • X.com Metrics: Handle, follower count (dated), engagement rate, content frequency, thread frequency, Spaces participation
  • YouTube Metrics: Channel name, subscriber count, average views (30–90 day), upload frequency, video duration, content category, audience demographics
  • Instagram Metrics: Handle, follower count, story/reel engagement rate, aesthetic style, content format distribution

Historical & Performance Data

  • Previous DeFi/crypto projects: Partnership timing, content formats, disclosed compensation
  • Audience response: Engagement patterns, conversion indicators, community sentiment
  • Project outcomes: Success/failure correlation, audience retention patterns
  • Contact information: Email, DM availability, agency representation
  • Promotional history: Transparent partnership tracking, performance metrics
